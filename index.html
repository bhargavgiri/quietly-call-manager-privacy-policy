Privacy first Mar 2026

🔒 100% local — Your contacts, calls & messages stay on your device. Never uploaded.

We built Quietly so you can control calls without giving up privacy. Every detail explained.

1. What we collect

  • Account: email & name (Firebase, encrypted)
  • Call & SMS: local only
  • Contacts: only for groups — stored on your phone
  • Volume changes: temporary during active group

2. Permissions

Every permission with WHY and HOW it's used:

READ_PHONE_STATE call state
Why?
Detect when phone is ringing
Usage?
Auto silent/vibrate based on your active group
No call recording, only state (ringing/idle)
READ_CONTACTS groups
Why?
Select people for quiet groups
Usage?
When creating "Family" or "Work" groups
Stays on your phone — never uploaded
SEND_SMS auto-reply
Why?
Send scheduled messages & auto-replies
Usage?
"In meeting, will call later" — you write it, we send it
Only sends messages you create
MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS
Why?
Silence phone during active groups
Usage?
Turns on silent/vibrate mode automatically
Restores settings when group ends
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM
Why?
Send at exact time you choose
Usage?
9:00 AM message goes at 9:00 AM sharp
No delays in scheduled messages
SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW
Why?
Show small button during calls
Usage?
Quickly see active group or change settings
Only UI overlay — no ads
BIND_SCREENING_SERVICE
Why?
Use Android's official call screening
Usage?
Identify saved numbers & apply group rules
Official Google API — secure
POST_NOTIFICATIONS
Why?
Alert you about active groups
Usage?
"Family group active — silent mode ON"
Can disable in Settings
INTERNET
Why?
Login, ads, premium purchases
Usage?
Firebase auth & AdMob (free version)
Call/message data never sent
BILLING
Why?
Premium subscriptions
Usage?
Secure payments via Google Play
We never see payment details
ANSWER_PHONE_CALLS
Why?
Call screening integration
Usage?
Apply group rules to incoming calls
No call content recorded
CALL_PHONE
Why?
Schedule call activation
Usage?
Activates group at your scheduled time
Only triggers when you schedule
MANAGE_OWN_CALLS
Why?
Telecom system integration
Usage?
Works with call screening for smooth experience
Required for call features
READ_PHONE_NUMBERS
Why?
Match incoming calls with groups
Usage?
Check if caller is in Family/Work group
Local check only
FOREGROUND_SERVICE_PHONE_CALL Android 14+
Why?
Keep call monitoring active in background
Usage?
Required for call screening on Android 14+
Auto-stops when you deactivate groups
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED
Why?
Restore schedules after phone restart
Usage?
Your scheduled messages work even after reboot
No data lost when phone restarts
IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS
Why?
Keep app running reliably during active groups
Usage?
Prevents Android from stopping call screening
Completely optional — you can disable
